ABOUT US

Hubble
Telescope

The Hubble Space Telescope is a large telescope in
space. It was launched into orbit by space shuttle
Discovery on April 24, 1990. Hubble orbits about 535
kilometers (332 miles) above Earth. It is the length
of a large school bus and weighs as much as two
adult elephants.
